# MacroActive - Company Overview

## Company Information

**Company Name:** MacroActive Limited
**Location:** Auckland, New Zealand
**Website:** https://www.macroactive.com/
**Contact:** info@macroactive.io

## Executive Summary

MacroActive is a New Zealand-based technology company that provides a white-label coaching platform specifically designed for online personal trainers and fitness creators. The company enables fitness professionals to scale their businesses by automating the delivery of personalized training programs and nutritional guidance through branded mobile applications.

## Mission, Vision & Values

### The Macro Mantra

- **Vision:** To help people live happier and healthier lives
- **Mission:** To help coaches grow successful businesses that impact more lives
- **Obsession:** To continuously innovate and add value to business partnerships with elite and passionate online fitness creators worldwide

## Core Product: The MacroActive Platform

### Overview

MacroActive provides fitness professionals with their own branded coaching platform (MacroApp) that automates business operations, allowing trainers to scale from handling dozens of clients to thousands without increasing administrative overhead.

### Key Features

#### 1. White-Label Branding
- 100% branded mobile apps (iOS/Android/WebApp)
- No "powered by" branding
- No links to competitive trainers
- Trainers retain full ownership of content and customer data
- Daily payout system for sales revenue

#### 2. Content Management
- **Training Programs:** Video coaching capabilities simulating in-person guidance
- **Nutrition & Meal Planning:**
  - Custom meal preferences (Mediterranean, Indian, Japanese, etc.)
  - Diet preferences (vegan, vegetarian, paleo, general, etc.)
  - Automated shopping list generation with family member scaling
  - Optional vegetarian days and re-feed days
  - Optional "treat" inclusions on select days

#### 3. Personalization Features
- Injury-aware exercise programming (flags exercises based on client injuries)
- Custom Spotify playlists for client workouts
- LIVE streaming capabilities
- VIP Coaching tiers
- Mindset content integration
- Supplement guide distribution
- One-click cross-selling of additional offers

#### 4. Customer Support
- Dedicated fitness professionals to handle client support
- Allows trainers to take time off without business interruption
- "Small army" of support staff for customer service
